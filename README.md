Machine learning researcher and engineer with experience in computer vision, natural language processing, graph ML, and time-series data analysis and forecasting. I am passionate about conducting research on new learning algorithms and building reliable ML systems. My research interests include online learning and transformers for sequence modeling, video prediction, and generative AI. I completed a Ph.D. at the University of Tokyo on respiratory motion forecasting with RNNs trained using online learning algorithms and transformers. I have also investigated physics and AI R&D problems across multiple industries (oil & gas, finance, healthcare, and identity & security). I lived in Japan for more than six years before moving to the UK. I am happy to connect and exchange ideas with like-minded tech professionals as well as computer science and ML enthusiasts.
